Visualizing Sentiment of Company Earnings Calls

  • Instructions
  • Visualization

Left: In this visualization we give an overview of the average sentiment score for the 30 companies in the DJIA. For each company, we also show the relevance of its earnings calls by showing the correlation of its sentiment its financial measures (revenue, operating income and net income). Use the drop-down menus to select the desired measure and sector, and click on a circle to select a company to view on the right.

Right: Each circle represents a quarter. The connected scatterplot shows how financial measures vary with sentiment over time for the company you have selected. The direction of the thin grey line indicates the direction of time, starting from 2008Q1 and ending in 2017Q4. Use the drop-down menus to select the desired financial measure, and mouseover a quarter to see the most frequent and specific words.

Please try and complete the following user tasks:

  • User task 1: For the company you were given, compare how positive and relevant the sentiment of its earnings calls were relative to other companies in the Dow Jones.
  • User task 2: For the company you were given, observe how its sentiment score varied with its financial measures over time. Please observe the general pattern of the relationship, also noting the unusual quarters.
